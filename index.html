My name is Dylan Hong, and I have always cared deeply about animals and the environment. In particular, I have always had a deep love for the animals that humanity has pushed the closest to extinction. What I've always been interested in is the ways to conserve and protect these animals at the brink of extinction. This site is designed to bring awareness to animals at extinctions gate, their story and the ways in which we can help.

This page is focused primarily on ways that we can help the environment around us. I will focus on the Michigan environment and how we can protect and sustain the animals in the area particularly around Ann Arbor.

My previous jobs include Associate Professor at California State University and positions at NASA JPL, the Naval Research Lab, and the NSA.
